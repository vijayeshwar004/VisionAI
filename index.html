Actionable Suggestions

01

Shift the Horizon Line

The current horizon perfectly halves the image. Try cropping to place it on the upper or lower third. Since the grass holds beautiful texture, giving the land 2/3 of the frame can make it feel more grounded.

02

Enhance the Foreground

Getting physically lower to include a distinct foreground object (like a rock or flowers) close to the lens would drastically increase the sense of scale and depth.

03

Tweak Contrast to Guide the Eye

While the moody lighting is great, it's slightly flat. Add a subtle vignette in editing to force the eye center-ward. A bump in contrast can also separate the grass from the mountains better.
